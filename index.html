  2. Prepare the meat sauce:
    1. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned.
    2. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, water, and Italian seasoning.
  3. Prepare the cheese mixture:
    1. In a separate bowl, mix together ricotta cheese and parsley.
  4. Layer & bake
